Why Should You Volunteer?

GoodFood volunteering program has been going on for almost a decade since 2011. Our people have been doing their duties with the highest level of compassion,care and professionalism.

By volunteering with us, you are helping to make, pack and distribute meals to thousands of people who are in need while helping us to end the hunger problems in Malaysia. Your roles in this volunteer program can be varient from picking up food items, packing and redistributing. We are welcoming any volunteer that want to enhance their skills in any ways while help us to promote, create awereness and along with campaign on food collection for us. We are very honoured to have these roles in our food volunteer program :

  • Planner
  • Cook
  • Distributer
  • Packer
  • Photographer
Volunteer Guidelines
  • Volunteers must be 18 years and above
  • 17 years old and below must fill up a consent form by their parents
  • Volunteers must arrive early to avoid any distraction
  • Everyone must wear masks due to this covid-19 situation
  • Maintain 6 feet distance while working
  • Please dress for the appropriate occasion
