About Us



Who are we?



GoodFood is an organization that collects donation from any party whether it is from public,restaurants,caterers and any large manufacturers. We are commited to raise awareness and donation in relation to SDG, Goal 2: Zero Hunger.

GoodFood also functions as distributor from the donation that we received. As a distributor, we distribute the food donation and places that has been required. Furthermore,the money donation that we receive will be used to buy.make food to be given to the needy.

In relation to Sustainable Development Goals
